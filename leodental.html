Located in the Joe V Shopping Center

Our goal at Leo Dentistry is to provide you and your family with comprehensive dental, periodontal, pediatric dentistry and orthodontic services in one convenient location.

In addition to being specialists in their fields, our dentists employ the latest techniques and procedures to ensure your comfort and results. We offer Invisalign orthodontics, laser tooth whitening, digital x-ray technology and the newest replacement for missing teeth- dental implants.

If you have questions, or would like to schedule a cleaning or consultation, our friendly staff will be happy to help.

We accept most insurances and are conveniently located in Joe V Smart Shop Shopping Center across the street from the San Jacinto Community College North

We look forward to the opportunity to help you and your entire family smile brightly!
