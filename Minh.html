Dr. Minh Phan

Dr. Rahul Joshi was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. His background in dentistry started very early as both of his parents are also practicing dentists. He studied and received his B.S. in Biological Sciences degree at University of Pacific in Stockton, California. Dr. Joshi continued with University of Pacific in San Francisco to earn his D.D.S. degree.

Since graduating, Dr. Joshi has had extensive experience in both the pediatric and surgical aspects of dentistry. Dr. Joshi has also been on mission trips around the world including locations like Costa Rica and the Dominican Republic to provide needed dental services for the impoverished of the area.

When he’s not in the office, Dr. Joshi spends much of his free time at the gym, traveling, or learning how to cook.
