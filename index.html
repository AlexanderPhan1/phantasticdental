We are committed to providing every member of your family with affordable, top-quality dental care. Our goal is to create healthy, beautiful smiles and develop lifelong relationships with you and your family. We are proud that the majority of our patients come from referrals from family members and "word by mouth". Dr.Phan works hard to ensure that every patient feels comfortable and welcome. We offer a complete range of dental services for patients of all ages, including General, Pediatric, Cosmetic, Orthodontics/Braces, and Emergency Dentistry. We can't wait to meet you!

From the time you walk in to the time you leave with a smile, we strive to create a warm and comforting environment. Our friendly staff makes every effort to make patients feel comfortable and welcomed. We strive to restore patients to optimal oral health and treat everyone as we would our own families.

At Phantastic Dental & Orthodontics, we are in the business of creating and maintaining healthy, beautiful smiles for the communities of Pasadena and Uvalde.
