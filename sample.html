Welcome to Fresh Dental Care, a leading provider in state-of-the-art cosmetic & general dentistry.

Fresh Dental Care's ethos is to provide the very best in patient care in an environment that is relaxing and inviting. Taking care of your needs is our mission. When you visit any of our convenient locations, your smile is our top priority. Our entire team is dedicated to providing you with the personalized and gentle care that you deserve. We pride ourselves on our ability to give you A New Reason to Smile. 

Fresh Dental Care believes in minimally invasive cosmetic dentistry. This means that our priority is to improve the look of your teeth without compromising their long term health. Part of our commitment to serving our patients includes providing information that helps them to make more informed decisions about their oral health needs. Whatever your dental needs may be, we will provide you with the latest, up-to-date solutions. A few examples include low radiation digital x-rays, anxiety control sedation, ultrasonic cleanings, metal-free crowns & bridges, natural tooth-colored mercury-free fillings, flexible and lightweight dentures & partials, porcelain veneers, composite bonding for gaps, power whitening, etc.

It is the goal of Fresh Dental Care to make dentistry affordable for everyone. With this in mind, we offer a competitive, set pricing for all of your dental needs. We constantly price check locally to ensure that we are one of the most affordable dental office in town. In addition, we do offer a Best Price Guarantee program that is the only one of its kind in the Houston area.

To arrange a Free, No Obligation Consultation & Estimate, please visit us at any of our convenient locations. All Fresh Dental Care locations are situated in-town, easily accessible by several major freeways and streets. We welcome new patients from the surrounding areas in Houston; including Bellaire, Conroe, Humble, Katy, Sugar Land, The Woodlands and more.
